What Every Feature Does

Website Structure

The pages that make up your site — home, services, service areas, and more. Google ranks individual pages, not just websites, so the more targeted pages you have, the more searches you show up for. Lite covers the essentials with 5 pages. Growth and Pro go deeper with expanded service and city pages that target specific local searches.

Mobile-First Design

Most homeowners searching for tree services are on their phone — often in the moment after a storm, a downed branch, or a hazard they just noticed. Your site is built to load fast and look perfect on mobile first, because a slow or broken experience on phone means they're calling someone else.

Tap-to-Call Buttons

Tree service leads are high intent. When someone's ready to call, a single tap connects them directly to your phone — no digging for a number, no extra steps, no reason to bounce.

Local Search Structure

Your site is built with the technical and content structure Google needs to understand where you work and what you do. Without it, Google guesses — and guesses wrong. With it, you show up when someone in your service area searches for exactly what you offer.

Google Business Profile Support

Your Google Business Profile is often the first thing a homeowner sees before they ever visit your website. We set it up, optimize it, and keep it active so your listing stays competitive — because an incomplete or ignored profile loses calls to whoever maintains theirs.

Review Request Page

Reviews drive calls, and most happy customers won't leave one unless you make it effortless. This is a simple dedicated page with a direct link to your Google review profile — remove every excuse and the reviews follow.

Website Updates

Your site stays current with ongoing edits to text, photos, service details, phone numbers, and anything else that changes. A stale site signals a dead business — keeping content fresh builds trust with visitors and tells Google your site is active and worth ranking.

Ongoing Page Improvement

A new service or city page added to your site every month on a consistent schedule. Every page targets a specific search — tree removal in one city, stump grinding in another, emergency service in a third — and compounds over time. This is how you grow your local footprint without running ads.

Call Tracking

A tracked phone number on your site that records call volume, duration, and caller data so you know exactly how many leads your website is generating. You can't improve what you don't measure.

Missed-Call Textback

When someone calls and you don't answer, they automatically get a text from your business within minutes. Tree service customers call multiple companies — an instant text keeps you in the conversation until you can call back, instead of losing the job to whoever responded first.

Google Search Campaigns

Paid ads that put your business at the top of Google when someone searches for tree services in your area. SEO builds over time — ads put you in front of buyers today. Running both means you're capturing leads now while your organic presence grows underneath.

Local Services Ads (Google Guaranteed)

A Google program that places your business above standard ads with a Google Guaranteed badge — and you only pay per lead, not per click. The badge is one of the strongest trust signals in local search. Homeowners see it and know Google has vetted your business, and you don't pay unless someone actually contacts you.
