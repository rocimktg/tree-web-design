Place the following assets in /assets:

- hero.jpg: Lifestyle/technician photo, 1600x1000+ JPEG, centered subject, neutral colors, optimized for fast loading.
- logo.svg: Simple neutral wordmark or icon; transparent background.
- service icons (optional): Monochrome SVGs, 48x48px, to replace inline placeholders.
- social icons (optional): SVGs for FB/IG/IN, 32x32px, single-color.
- favicon.ico or favicon.svg (optional): 32x32px square mark.
