Straight Answers.

Everything tree service pros ask before signing on.

What plans do you offer?

We offer three website options with no setup fees on monthly plans.

Lite ($125/mo, 6-month agreement): A focused one-page website with hosting, SSL, basic on-page SEO and local schema, a Review Request Page, Google Business Profile setup or audit, and operational edits plus an annual refresh.

Growth ($249/mo, 12-month agreement): A 5-page managed website with expanded on-page SEO and local schema, a Review Request Page, monthly light Google Business Profile activity, hosting, SSL, unlimited operational edits, and two new or improved pages added per month. This is the most popular option.

Lump-Sum ($3,500 one-time): A 10-page custom website build. You pay once and own the finished site files at completion. Hosting is optional at $25/mo. Ongoing edits are optional at $50/mo. Google Business Profile setup is available as an add-on.

Additional service or city pages are $100 each on any plan.

How many pages do I get?

Lite launches with a one-page website covering your services, service area, photos, trust signals, reviews, contact form, and click-to-call.

Growth launches with 5 core pages, then adds two new or improved pages per month over the course of your agreement.

Lump-Sum includes 10 pages total built at launch.

Additional service or city pages are $100 each on any plan. Ongoing monthly page work on Growth does not roll over.

How does billing work?

There are no setup fees on monthly plans.

Your first monthly payment is due at kickoff to start the build. Lite has a 6-month agreement. Growth has a 12-month agreement. Lump-Sum is a one-time payment of $3,500 due at project start.

Monthly plans continue month-to-month after the initial term unless you cancel or choose to transfer files.

Recurring payments are handled automatically through Stripe on the same billing date each month.

Do I own the website?

With the Lump-Sum option, yes. You pay once and own the finished website files after launch. Host it wherever you want, or add optional hosting at $25/mo through Tree Web Design.

Lite and Growth are managed website service plans, which means hosting, maintenance, support, and the live website are included while your plan is active. Your domain, logo, photos, and written copy are always yours.

What happens after my initial term?

The plan continues month-to-month. Nothing changes — your site stays live, edits continue, and all included services continue.

If you want to stop, you can cancel before your next billing date or request a buyout quote to take ownership of the finished site files.

What happens if I cancel Lite or Growth?

Your monthly website service ends, and the live website is taken offline after the agreement or cancellation period. Your domain, logo, photos, and written copy remain yours.

If you want to keep the site, the Lump-Sum option is the best fit — or you can request a buyout quote at any point after your initial term.

Can I buy out the site later?

Yes. Monthly plan clients can request a buyout quote after their initial term if they want to move from a managed plan to full website ownership.

Buyout prices: Lite — $2,500 / Growth — $3,500. Buyout includes the finished site files. Hosting and support are separate after buyout.

What is the Review Request Page?

Every plan includes a Review Request Page at your domain, such as yourbusiness.com/feedback.

You send customers the link or a QR code after the job.

Happy customers can leave a public review on Google, Yelp, Nextdoor, or other platforms.

If something was off, they can contact you privately first. See Demo.

What do you need from me to get started?

Not much. Once you're ready to move forward, we'll need your logo, a few photos of your work, and basic business info — services, service area, contact details. If you don't have professional photos yet, we'll work with what you have. We handle everything else from there.

Can you write the copy for my site?

Yes. You don't need to write a word. We'll pull what we need from our kickoff call — your services, your market, what makes you different — and write copy that sounds like a real tree service pro, not a template.

How fast will my site launch?

Once we have your payment and materials — logo, business info, photos — we get to work. Most sites are live within 7–10 business days.

Do I have to commit before seeing the work?

No. Before you pay anything, you'll see a working homepage built around your business — your services, your market, your name on it. Not a mockup or a template with your logo swapped in. A real, hand-coded page.

If it doesn't look right or feel like a fit, you walk away with nothing owed. Most people are ready to move forward after seeing it.

Why don't you use WordPress or page builders?

WordPress sites rely on plugins that break and create security risks. Page builders bloat your code and slow your site down. We hand-code everything from scratch. The result is a site that loads in under a second, never breaks on its own, and has nothing for hackers to exploit.

Do you manage Google Profiles?

Yes. Google Business Profile support is included with all plans.

Lite includes a one-time setup or audit to make sure your profile is complete and aligned with your website.

Growth includes monthly light Google Business Profile activity to keep your listing current.

Lump-Sum — Profile setup is available as an add-on.

What does ongoing page work mean?

It means we add or improve service, service-area, or specialty pages over time based on what people are searching for and what your business wants to target.

Some months that may mean a new page. Other months it may mean improving an existing page.

Growth includes two new or improved pages per month. Unused monthly page work does not roll over.

What counts as an operational edit?

Operational edits are factual business updates — phone numbers, hours, address, service area, links, and form details. The kind of thing that changes when your business information changes.

General copy, text rewrites, and photo swaps are part of unlimited edits on Growth, not operational edits. Lite includes operational edits plus one annual refresh.

New pages, new sections, layout changes, and redesigns are not included in either — those are handled through monthly page work on Growth or quoted separately.

Are edits really unlimited on Growth?

Yes. Growth includes unlimited edits to existing pages — copy, text, photos, business info, links, and forms. If it lives on the site already, it can be updated.

New pages, new sections, and redesigns are separate. Those are handled through the two monthly pages included with Growth or quoted individually.

Do tree service companies really need a website?

Yes. Tree work is high-trust, high-ticket, and often urgent. Homeowners compare companies quickly, especially on mobile. A fast, professional website helps you look credible, explain your services, and make it easy to call.

How do I rank my tree service on Google Maps?

Google Maps ranking comes down to three things: a complete and active Google Business Profile, a fast website, and consistent five-star reviews.

Your Google Business Profile needs accurate service categories, real job photos, and regular updates. Your website needs to load fast, mention your city and services clearly, and link to your Google Business Profile. Reviews are the multiplier — the more recent and relevant, the higher you climb.

Lite includes a one-time Google Profile setup or audit. Growth includes monthly light Google Business Profile activity to keep your listing current.

Is Lite just a single-page website?

Yes. Lite is a one-page website for tree companies that need a professional online presence without a larger SEO buildout. It covers the essentials: services, service area, trust signals, reviews, contact form, and click-to-call. It also includes Google Business Profile setup or audit and a Review Request Page.

If you want dedicated service pages, service-area pages, and ongoing local search expansion, Growth is the better fit.

The Lump-Sum plan is a one-time project starting at $3,500. We build a 10-page custom website and you own the finished files at completion. There are no monthly fees unless you add optional hosting at $25/mo or ongoing edits at $50/mo. This option is best for companies that prefer to pay once and own the site outright.

Is hosting included?

Yes, hosting and SSL are included with Lite and Growth at no extra charge.

For the Lump-Sum option, hosting is available after launch as an optional add-on at $25/mo.

Do monthly page improvements roll over?

No. Unused monthly page work on Growth does not roll over to the following month.
