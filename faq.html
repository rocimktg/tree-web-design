Straight Answers.

Everything tree service pros ask before signing on. Month-to-month after the initial term, clear ownership rules, and buyout available if you ever want the website files transferred.

What plans do you offer?

We offer three website plans with no setup fees.

Lite ($149/mo, 3-month agreement): A custom single-page website with hosting, edits, and the core setup you need to start strong.

Growth ($349/mo, 6-month agreement): Everything in Lite plus 5 core pages, 5 custom city pages, monthly Google Profile Management, and ongoing SEO expansion.

Pro ($549/mo, 12-month agreement): Everything in Growth plus 10 custom city pages, active weekly Google Profile Management, and Google Ads / LSA support. Ad spend is separate.

All plans include hosting, SSL, unlimited website updates for text, photos, and content changes, and a $0 setup fee.

How many pages do I get?

Lite includes a single-page site.

Growth includes 5 core pages plus 5 custom city pages.

Pro includes 5 core pages plus 10 custom city pages (15 total).

Additional pages are $100 each, one-time.

How does billing work?

There are no setup fees on any plan.

Your first monthly payment is due at kickoff to start the build. Lite has a 3-month agreement, Growth has a 6-month agreement, and Pro has a 12-month agreement.

After the initial term, your plan simply continues month-to-month unless you cancel, change plans, or choose a buyout option.

Recurring payments are handled automatically through Stripe on the same billing date each month.

Do I own my website?

Your domain, logo, photos, and written copy are yours.

The live website runs on Tree Web Design's managed hosting while your monthly plan is active.

If you stay on the plan after the initial term, nothing changes. Your website stays live, hosting continues, edits continue, support continues, and your included plan services continue.

Can I buy out my website?

Yes. After your initial term, the normal option is to keep your monthly plan going. If you want to stop the monthly plan and take the site elsewhere, you can choose the buyout option.

For Lite, the buyout is $2,000 after the 3-month agreement.

For Growth, the buyout is $0 after the 6-month agreement.

For Pro, the buyout is $0 after the 12-month agreement.

For Growth and Pro, the buyout cost is $0, but you still need to choose the buyout option if you want the finished website files transferred to you.

The buyout transfers the finished website files to you. From there, you can host the site wherever you want, or keep it hosted with Tree Web Design for $25/month.

What happens after my initial term?

The normal option is to continue month-to-month.

If you stay on the plan, your website stays live, hosting continues, edits continue, support continues, and your included plan services continue.

If you want to stop the monthly plan, you can either choose the buyout option or let the managed site come offline.

What happens if I cancel?

After your initial term, you can cancel before your next billing date.

If you cancel and do not choose buyout, the managed website comes offline at the end of your billing period.

No website files are transferred without buyout.

Your domain, logo, photos, and written copy remain yours.

What is the Review Request Page?

Every plan includes a Review Request Page at your domain, such as yourbusiness.com/feedback.

You send customers the link or a QR code after the job.

Happy customers can leave a public review on Google, Yelp, Nextdoor, or other platforms.

If something was off, they can contact you privately first. See Demo.

What do you need from me to get started?

Not much. Once you're ready to move forward, we'll need your logo, a few photos of your work, and basic business info — services, service area, contact details. If you don't have professional photos yet, we'll work with what you have. We handle everything else from there.

Can you write the copy for my site?

Yes. You don't need to write a word. We'll pull what we need from our kickoff call — your services, your market, what makes you different — and write copy that sounds like a real tree service pro, not a template.

How fast will my site launch?

Once we have your payment and materials — logo, business info, photos — we get to work. Most sites are live within 7–10 business days.

Why don't you use WordPress or page builders?

WordPress sites rely on plugins that break and create security risks. Page builders bloat your code and slow your site down. We hand-code everything from scratch. The result is a site that loads in under a second, never breaks on its own, and has nothing for hackers to exploit.

Do you manage Google Profiles?

Yes. Google Profile support is built into all three plans, and ads support is included with Pro only.

Lite includes one-time setup to make sure your profile is complete and aligned with your website.

Growth includes monthly Google Profile Management.

Pro includes active weekly Google Profile Management, plus Google Ads / LSA support with setup, tracking, and monthly review. Ad spend is separate.

Do tree service companies really need a website?

Yes — and a website built to generate calls matters more for tree service than most trades. Tree work is high-ticket and high-trust. Homeowners aren't just hiring the cheapest option; they're hiring whoever looks most credible when they search. A slow or outdated site — or no site at all — hands those jobs to whoever shows up next in Google.

Beyond credibility, a well-built site works for you 24/7. It captures leads after hours, during storms, and on weekends when your phone is off. The tree service pros getting the most calls aren't always the best in their market. They're just the most visible online.

How do I rank my tree service on Google Maps?

Google Maps ranking comes down to three things: a complete and active Google Business Profile, a fast website, and consistent five-star reviews.

Your Google Business Profile needs accurate service categories, real job photos, and regular updates. Your website needs to load fast, mention your city and services clearly, and link to your Google Business Profile. Reviews are the multiplier — the more recent and relevant, the higher you climb.

Lite includes one-time profile setup. Growth includes monthly Google Profile Management. Pro includes active weekly Google Profile Management — we keep your profile current with photos and posts from jobs you send us.
