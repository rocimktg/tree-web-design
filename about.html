The Story

Websites Built With Purpose & Intent

I spent a decade at a national company learning how digital presence drives revenue at scale. Then I looked around at local contractors and saw the same problems everywhere — slow sites, template designs, and nobody to call when something broke.

Rocinante Marketing exists to fix that.

I'm JJ Jaggers, owner and developer at Rocinante Marketing. I spent a decade at Federal Heath, a national sign manufacturer, working my way from Art Director to Digital Marketing Manager. In that time I rebuilt the company's digital footprint across five divisions, owned their advertising and lead generation strategy, and learned firsthand how visibility and execution drive results at scale.

What I noticed along the way was that local service businesses — the contractors, tradespeople, and small operators — rarely had access to that level of attention. Most were stuck with template sites, slow load times, and no one to call when something needed to change.

Rocinante Marketing helps home service contractors look professional online without adding extra work. We build fast, hand-coded websites designed to perform, not just sit there.

We handle your online presence like a dedicated digital manager — keeping your site updated, managing your Google Business profile, and making sure everything runs smoothly.
