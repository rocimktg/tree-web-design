The Story

Websites Built With Purpose & Intent

I spent a decade at a national company learning how digital presence drives revenue at scale. Then I looked around at local tree service pros and saw the same problems everywhere — slow sites, template designs, and nobody to call when something broke.

Tree Web Design exists to fix that.

I'm JJ Jaggers, owner and developer at Tree Web Design. I spent a decade at Federal Heath, a national sign manufacturer, working my way from Art Director to Digital Marketing Manager. In that time I rebuilt the company's digital footprint across five divisions, owned their advertising and lead generation strategy, and learned firsthand how visibility and execution drive results at scale.

What I noticed along the way was that local service businesses — the tree service pros, tradespeople, and small operators — rarely had access to that level of attention. Most were stuck with template sites, slow load times, and no one to call when something needed to change.

Tree Web Design specializes in tree service web design — fast, hand-coded websites that help tree service companies look professional online and get more calls without adding extra work.

We handle your online presence like a dedicated digital manager — keeping your site updated, managing your Google Business profile, and making sure everything runs smoothly.
